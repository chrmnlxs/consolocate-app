Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Room 201 located? ‚ñº

Room 201 is located on the second floor of the Main Building, near the library entrance. Take the main staircase and turn right.

How do I find my class schedule? ‚ñº

Class schedules can be obtained from the Registrar's Office or accessed through the student portal online at portal.lcup.edu.ph

What is CONSOLOCATE? ‚ñº

CONSOLOCATE is an interactive 2D campus navigation system with LED path guidance, designed to help students, faculty, and visitors navigate La Consolacion University Philippines easily.

Where is the Registrar's Office? ‚ñº

The Registrar's Office is located in the Administration Building, Ground Floor, Room 105. Office hours are Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM.

How do I use the LED path navigation? ‚ñº

After selecting your destination, click "Show LED Path" to activate the physical LED lights on our 3D campus model. Follow the illuminated path from your current location to your destination.
